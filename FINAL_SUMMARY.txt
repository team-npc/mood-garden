PHILOSOPHY IMPLEMENTATION

BEFORE (Quantification-Focused):
• Numbers everywhere (progress, percentages, points, streaks)
• Pressure-creating metrics
• Obligation-based language ("Challenges")
• Time pressure (countdown timers)
• Reward display creates external motivation
• Difficulty hierarchy creates intimidation

AFTER (Qualitative-Focused):
• No visible numbers in UI
• Qualitative feedback ("keep going", "ready to complete")
• Invitation-based language ("Invitations")
• No time pressure ("fresh invitations daily")
• Rewards hidden (backend support maintained)
• Equal invitation without hierarchy

RESULT: Mindful, pressure-free user experience with full internal functionality
