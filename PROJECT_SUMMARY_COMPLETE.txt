Name:           Mood Garden
Version:        2.0
Type:           Mindful Journaling Web Application
Core Concept:   Virtual plant grows with your journal reflections

A beautiful, anxiety-free journaling experience where your thoughts nurture a virtual plant
that evolves naturally through 6 growth stages — no statistics, no pressure, just organic
growth and gentle encouragement for consistent self-reflection.

Database Structure:

/users/{userId}
  ├── /profile                    → User metadata
  │     ├── uid: string
  │     ├── displayName: string
  │     ├── email: string
  │     ├── photoURL: string
  │     ├── createdAt: timestamp
  │     └── preferences: object
  │
  ├── /plant                      → Plant state
  │     ├── stage: string         (seed/sprout/plant/blooming/tree/fruitingTree)
  │     ├── health: number        (0-100, internal only)
  │     ├── lastWatered: timestamp
  │     ├── lastEntryDate: timestamp
  │     ├── currentStreak: number (internal)
  │     ├── totalEntries: number  (internal)
  │     ├── flowers: array
  │     ├── fruits: array
  │     └── customization: object
  │
  └── /entries/{entryId}          → Journal entries
        ├── content: string       (encrypted at rest)
        ├── mood: string
        ├── tags: array
        ├── createdAt: timestamp
        ├── isPinned: boolean
        ├── isPrivate: boolean
        ├── attachments: array
        └── metadata: object

Security Rules:
• Users can only read/write their own data
• All entries are private by default
• Server-side timestamps prevent time manipulation
• Batch operations limited to prevent abuse

Performance:
• Pagination: 20 entries per page
• Indexes on createdAt, mood, tags
• Lazy loading for heavy components
• Memoized selectors for expensive calculations

🌿 HOW PLANT GROWTH WORKS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Growth Stages (Qualitative Display):
1. Seed          → "Beginning your journey"           (tiny pot with soil)
2. Sprout        → "First green shoots emerging"      (small sprout)
3. Young Plant   → "Growing steadily"                 (multiple leaves)
4. Blooming      → "Beautiful flowers appearing"      (buds + flowers)
5. Tree          → "Strong and established"           (trunk + canopy)
6. Fruiting Tree → "Abundant and thriving"            (flowers + fruits)

Internal Logic (Hidden from User):
• Entries contribute "growth points"
• Consistency affects health (0-100)
• Time since last entry impacts wilting
• But user NEVER sees these numbers

Health Decay:
• Day 0 (new entry):   Health +10%, fully restored
• Day 1:               Maintained
• Day 2:               -5% health
• Days 3-6:            -8% health per day
• Day 7+:              -10% health per day
• Plant never dies permanently, just needs care

Visual States (User-Facing):
✨ Thriving       → Vibrant, animated, glowing

Mood Garden 2.0 is more than a journaling app—it's a gentle companion for your
mental health journey. By removing anxiety-inducing metrics and embracing natural
growth metaphors, we've created a space where reflection feels nurturing, not
stressful.

Every word you write waters your plant. Every mood you track adds color to your
garden. Every moment of consistency helps your tree grow stronger. But it's not
about the numbers—it's about showing up for yourself.

Your plant may wilt when you're away, but it will always recover when you return.
Just like real growth, there's no judgment, no pressure, no competition. Only
gentle encouragement to keep cultivating your inner garden.

Remember: Your mental health journey is unique. Mood Garden is a tool to support
your reflection practice, not replace professional mental health care when needed.
